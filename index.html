About the Project

India’s digital space is fast-moving—and increasingly vulnerable. Misinformation spreads quickly, fueling confusion, fear, and division. The impact is real: fractured communities, public health risks, and financial harm.

NeuraLens is our response.
A poetic, intelligent lens powered by Generative AI—built to detect, educate, and restore trust.

We’re not just fact-checking. We’re crafting a companion that helps users pause and ask: “Is this true?”

By guiding people to recognize credible sources and understand context, NeuraLens nurtures a more thoughtful, informed digital citizenry.

Because truth isn’t just data.
It’s discernment.
It’s responsibility.
It’s human.
